## How It Works

### For Athletes ğŸš´

1. **Connect** your Strava account (one time, takes 30 seconds)
2. **Ride** on the segment of the week during the event day
3. **Watch** the leaderboard update automatically
4. **Earn** points to compete for the season championship

### For Admins ğŸ“‹

1. **Create** a new weekly challenge with a Strava segment
2. **Announce** the event date and time to your club
3. **Fetch** results after the event day ends
4. **Share** the leaderboard with your community

## Features at a Glance

| Feature | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Automatic Sync** | Strava activities sync automatically during event windows |
| **Best Activity Selection** | Only your fastest qualifying activity counts each week |
