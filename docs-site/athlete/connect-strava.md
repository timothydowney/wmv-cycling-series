## Step-by-Step

### 1. Start the Connection
Go to [wmv-cycling-series-production.up.railway.app](https://wmv-cycling-series-production.up.railway.app) and look for the **"Connect with Strava"** button.

![Alt: Connection button location on homepage]

Click it.

### 2. Review Permissions
You'll be taken to Strava's website. A screen will show:
- **WMV Cycling Series** is requesting permission
- We're asking for: "Activity access" and "Profile information"

This is normal! We need these to see your segment data.

### 3. Authorize
Click the blue **"Authorize"** button.

![Alt: Strava authorization screen]

### 4. You're Connected!
You'll be sent back to WMV Cycling Series. You should see:
- Your name and profile picture
- A **"Disconnect"** button (if you ever want to unplug)

**Done!** Your Strava account is now connected.

## Troubleshooting Connection

### "Connection Failed" Error
- Make sure you clicked "Authorize" on Strava
- Try refreshing the page and connecting again
- Check that you're using the correct Strava password

### I Don't See the Connect Button
- Are you on the homepage? Look in the top navigation
- Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Check that you're not already connected (you'd see your name instead)

### I'm Already Connected But Can't See My Name
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Disconnect and reconnect

## After Connecting

Your account is now linked! Here's what happens next:

1. **Admin creates an event** - They'll announce a weekly segment challenge
2. **You ride the segment** - Complete it during the event window
3. **Results are fetched** - After the event, an admin collects all results
4. **You appear on the leaderboard** - Automatically!

You don't need to do anything special. Once connected, your Strava activities sync automatically during event windows.
